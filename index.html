About Me

Hi am Tonny Lumwaji Lime from Kenya,I current stay in Nairobi the capital city of kenya. .The reason I am in this city is because of the easy access of essential things like,education jobs,church and people people with good business ideas.

I came to the city in 2006 in search of a better school to continue my high school education.It was my Uncles idea to migrate to the city since he didn't see any good school in western Kenya my rural motherland.

In the city I was able to be introduced to missionaries and I join the church in 2007, the same year I left the city to Narok wher I joined Narok High School.

In 2010 I graduated from high school and return back to the city.I started preparing for mission and i was privileged to serve in South Africa Durban Mission from 2013 to 2015.

After my mission I joined collage and since 2017 I started going for interniship and managed to secure some jobs in Automotive industry as a vehicle diagnostic and wiring technician. I currently work at Subaru Kenya one of the vehicle company in Kenya.
