Website Planning Document

Site Name

Lumix Subaru Solenoids

The name represents a business specializing in Subaru transmission solenoids and repair support services in Kenya.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about Subaru transmission solenoids, common transmission problems, repair solutions, pricing information, and contact details for customers seeking affordable Subaru parts and support in Kenya.

Target Audience

Primary Audience

  • Subaru vehicle owners
  • Auto mechanics
  • Automotive repair shops
  • Car enthusiasts

User Needs

  • Find the correct Subaru solenoid.
  • Learn about transmission problems.
  • Get affordable pricing.
  • Contact a trusted supplier.

Scenarios

  • My Subaru transmission is jerking. Which solenoid might be faulty?
  • How can I contact Lumix Subaru Solenoids for support?
  • Do you stock Lock-Up and Duty Solenoids?
  • What are the prices of Subaru transmission solenoids?
